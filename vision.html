For fifty years, Nike's journey has been driven by a dream to serve athletes and make a positive impact in the world.We've championed women's rights in sports, tackled climate change, and confronted racial injustice. Despite challenges, our commitment to a bigger purpose remains unwavering. Nike's mission is to inspire and innovate for every athleteglobally, moving the world forward through sport's power. Mark Parker

Chairman

Mark Parker served as NIKE, Inc. Chairman, President & CEO for 14 years, Chairman for four years,
and continues to serve as Executive Chairman. Mark joined Nike in 1979 as one of the company’s first
footwear designers and has been at the center of Nike innovation ever since. He has brought innovative concepts
and engineering expertise into roles such as VP, Consumer Product Marketing; VP, Global Footwear; and
Co-President of the Nike Brand. Mark’s focus on innovation led the way for Nike Air and other industry-changing
breakthroughs in product design. Mark serves on the Board of Directors for The Walt Disney Company
and received a bachelor’s degree in political science from Penn State.
